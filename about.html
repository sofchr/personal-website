About Sofie

When I'm not coding, I can be found partaking in some other fun activities!


Music

I earned my Bachelor of Music degree in vocal performance, so you can find me most days belting out a tune at home or at gigs around NYC. I love to play piano, and am teaching myself to play the accordion.

Cooking

I was diagnosed with celiac disease in 2014. While it makes eating out more difficult, it fueled my passion for cooking! I love to create gluten-free versions of all my favorite dishes.

Crafting

I enjoy dabbling in craft projects of all kinds- embroidery, taxidermy, watercolor, sewing, you name it. I may not finish them half of the time but boy is it fun to start them!
